<p><p>The pigmented lesion treatment market features several key device types. Energy-based devices leverage concentrated energy to target and reduce unwanted pigmentation. RF (Radiofrequency) devices utilize radio waves to heat the skin, promoting collagen remodeling and reducing pigmentation. Ultrasound devices use sound waves to penetrate and treat deeper skin layers, improving skin tone. IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) devices emit varied wavelengths of light to target pigmented lesions, effectively breaking down melanin and enhancing skin appearance. These technologies collectively enhance cosmetic dermatology practices.</p></p>
