<p><p>Digital ballast is an advanced electronic device used in lighting systems, specifically for controlling high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps and fluorescent lights. Unlike traditional magnetic ballasts, digital ballasts enhance energy efficiency, improve light quality, and provide better control over lighting systems. This technology allows for features such as dimming and flicker-free operation, making it suitable for various applications, including commercial, industrial, and residential environments.</p><p>The Digital Ballast Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period.
