<p><p>The dog clippers market is categorized into three main types: mains type, cordless type, and hybrid type. Mains type clippers are powered directly from an outlet, offering consistent power but limited mobility. Cordless type clippers operate on rechargeable batteries, providing flexibility and ease of movement, making them ideal for home grooming or travel. Hybrid type clippers combine both functionalities, allowing users to plug in for continuous use or switch to battery mode, catering to various grooming needs and preferences.</p></p>
