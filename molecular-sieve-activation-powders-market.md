<p><strong>The Molecular Sieve Activation Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>X Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molecular sieve activation powders are categorized into two primary types: A Type and X Type. A Type molecular sieves are characterized by their ability to adsorb smaller molecules due to their uniform pore size of around 3 angstroms, making them suitable for removing water and other small contaminants. In contrast, X Type molecular sieves have larger pore sizes of about 10 angstroms, enabling them to adsorb larger molecules, such as hydrocarbons. Both types are utilized in various industries for purification and separation processes.</p></p>
