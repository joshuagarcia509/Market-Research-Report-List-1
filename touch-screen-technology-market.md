<p><p>Touch screen technology encompasses various types, including resistive, capacitive, and infrared touchscreens. Resistive touchscreens respond to pressure, making them suitable for environments where gloves or styluses are used. Capacitive touchscreens detect electrical charges, offering higher sensitivity and clarity, ideal for smartphones and tablets. Infrared touchscreens utilize light beams to detect touch, allowing for multi-touch capabilities. Surface acoustic wave displays use ultrasonic waves, providing high image quality and durability. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse user needs and preferences.</p></p>
