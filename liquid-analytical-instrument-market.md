<p><p>The Liquid Analytical Instrument market encompasses various types designed for monitoring and analyzing liquid quality. pH/ORP analyzers measure acidity and oxidation-reduction potential, crucial for chemical processes. Turbidity analyzers assess water clarity, indicating contamination levels. Dissolved oxygen analyzers gauge oxygen saturation, vital for aquatic life health. Ammonium analyzers determine nitrogen levels, important for wastewater treatment. Chlorine analyzers track disinfection efficacy in water systems. Near Infrared analyzers provide insights into liquid composition without physical contact, serving diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
