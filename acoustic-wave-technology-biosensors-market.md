<p><p>The Acoustic Wave Technology Biosensors market includes two primary types: Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensors and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors. QCM sensors measure mass changes on a quartz crystal surface, offering high sensitivity for detecting biomolecular interactions, making them ideal for applications in healthcare and environmental monitoring. SAW sensors utilize acoustic waves on a substrate to detect changes in frequency or amplitude with high precision, suitable for real-time monitoring of biochemical processes and pathogen detection in various fields.</p></p>
