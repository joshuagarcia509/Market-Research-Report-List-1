<p><strong>The Turret Truck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Battery Electric Turret Truck</li><li>LPG Turret Truck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turret truck market primarily comprises two types: battery electric turret trucks and LPG turret trucks. Battery electric turret trucks are powered by electric batteries, offering zero emissions, reduced operational costs, and lower noise levels, making them suitable for indoor environments. In contrast, LPG turret trucks utilize liquefied petroleum gas, providing quick refueling and longer operational hours, making them ideal for outdoor and heavy-duty applications. The choice between these types depends on operational needs, environmental considerations, and cost factors.</p></p>
