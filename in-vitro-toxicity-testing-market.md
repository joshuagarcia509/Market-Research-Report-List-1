<p><strong>The In-vitro Toxicity Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Well Plate System</li><li>Multi-compartmental Perfused Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-vitro toxicity testing encompasses various market types, including static well plate systems and multi-compartmental perfused systems. Static well plate systems involve cells cultured in fixed conditions, enabling straightforward toxicity assessments through exposure to test compounds. In contrast, multi-compartmental perfused systems mimic in vivo environments by allowing continuous flow of nutrients and waste removal, providing more dynamic and realistic interactions between cells and toxicants. These systems enhance the predictive accuracy of toxicity results, fostering safer pharmaceutical and chemical development.</p></p>
