<p><p>Cell separation by membrane filtration technology involves various types of membrane filters, each suited for specific applications. Nylon membrane filters are known for their robustness and chemical compatibility. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) membranes excel in aggressive chemical environments. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) membranes offer high tensile strength and low extractables. Other membranes include specialty filters tailored for unique separation needs.</p></p>
