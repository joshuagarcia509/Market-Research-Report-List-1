<p><strong>The Smart Range Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Suction</li><li>Direct Suction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart range hood market comprises two primary types: side suction and direct suction hoods. Side suction hoods are designed to draw smoke and odors from the sides, making them ideal for kitchen layouts where overhead space is limited. Direct suction hoods, on the other hand, feature a vertical extraction system that captures airborne particles directly above the cooking surface, ensuring efficient removal of smoke and grease. Both types leverage smart technology for enhanced performance and user convenience.</p></p>
