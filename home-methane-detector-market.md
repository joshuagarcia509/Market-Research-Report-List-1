<p><ul><li> Catalytic Combustion Sensor</li><li>Semiconductor Sensor </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home methane detector market offers various sensor types for effective methane detection. Catalytic combustion sensors work by oxidizing methane over a catalyst, generating heat and triggering an alarm when gas levels exceed safety thresholds. In contrast, semiconductor sensors utilize a sensing element that changes resistance in the presence of methane, providing a quicker and often more compact solution. Both sensor types are crucial for enhancing home safety by detecting potentially hazardous methane leaks promptly and efficiently.</p></p>
