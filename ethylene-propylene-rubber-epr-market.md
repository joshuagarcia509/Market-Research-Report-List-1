<p><ul><li>Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM)</li><li>EPDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR) encompasses two main types: Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM is a solid rubber designed for high-impact applications, offering excellent resistance to heat, aging, and ozone. EPDM, on the other hand, contains diene; it enhances chemical stability and elasticity, making it suitable for outdoor applications and automotive parts. Both EPR types are prized for their durability, flexibility, and resistance to environmental degradation, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
