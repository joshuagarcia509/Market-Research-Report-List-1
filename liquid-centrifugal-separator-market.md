<p><ul><li>Filtration Centrifuge</li><li>Sedimentation Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid centrifugal separator market comprises two main types: filtration centrifuges and sedimentation centrifuges. Filtration centrifuges utilize a porous media to separate solid particles from liquids, making them ideal for applications requiring clear liquid outputs. In contrast, sedimentation centrifuges rely on gravitational forces and centrifugal action to separate solids based on density differences, often used for thicker suspensions. Both types play crucial roles across industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment, enhancing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
