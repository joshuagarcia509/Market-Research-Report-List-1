<p><p>The RT-PCR technology market is divided into two main types: Real-time Fluorescence Detection and No Real-time Fluorescence Detection. Real-time Fluorescence Detection employs fluorescent dyes to monitor amplification in real time, allowing for precise quantification of RNA levels. This method is widely used in diagnostics and research due to its accuracy and speed. In contrast, No Real-time Fluorescence Detection relies on endpoint analysis, typically providing qualitative results, making it suitable for applications where quantitative data is not critical.</p></p>
