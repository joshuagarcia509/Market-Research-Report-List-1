<p><p>The loom machine market comprises various types catering to different weaving needs. Floor looms are large, versatile machines ideal for complex projects. Inkle looms specifically weave narrow bands, while tapestry looms focus on intricate wall hangings. Card weaving involves using cards for patterns, appealing to traditional techniques.
