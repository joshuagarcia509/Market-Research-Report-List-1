<p><p>The vertical foldable mobile phone market encompasses various technologies, primarily focusing on two key types: Continuous Processing Interleaved (CPI) and Ultra-Thin Glass (UTG). CPI involves a technique that uses flexible OLED displays and special polymer layers for better durability and visual performance. In contrast, UTG employs exceptionally thin glass to provide a premium feel while enhancing scratch resistance. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing flexibility, aesthetics, and functionality in foldable designs.</p></p>
