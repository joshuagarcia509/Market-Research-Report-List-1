<p><p>The fire beam detectors market primarily includes infrared beam detectors and optical beam detectors. Infrared beam detectors use heat signatures to identify smoke or flames, operating effectively in large spaces. They emit infrared beams and detect disturbances caused by smoke particles. Optical beam detectors, on the other hand, utilize light beams to sense smoke presence, making them suitable for environments where traditional smoke detectors may not be effective. Both types provide early warning systems for fire safety in various commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
