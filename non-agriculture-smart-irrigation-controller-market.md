<p><ul><li>Weather-based Controllers</li><li>Sensor-based Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Agriculture Smart Irrigation Controller market comprises two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to optimize irrigation schedules, reducing water waste by adjusting watering based on rainfall and temperature forecasts. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors to measure the actual moisture levels in the ground, triggering irrigation only when needed. Both types enhance water efficiency and promote sustainable landscape management in non-agricultural settings.</p></p>
