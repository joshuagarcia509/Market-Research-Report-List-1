<p><ul><li>HV Mobile Substation</li><li>LV/MV Mobile Substation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile substation market is categorized into two primary types: High Voltage (HV) and Low/Medium Voltage (LV/MV) mobile substations. HV mobile substations are designed to manage high voltage transmission, facilitating swift load transfers and grid stabilization during outages or maintenance. In contrast, LV/MV mobile substations cater to lower voltage applications, providing temporary power supply solutions for industrial and commercial sites. Both types enhance flexibility, minimize downtime, and support infrastructure in emergency situations and planned projects.</p></p>
