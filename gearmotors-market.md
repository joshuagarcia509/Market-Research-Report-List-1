<p><ul><li>Helical</li><li>Helical-Bevel</li><li>Planetary</li><li>Worm</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gearmotors are classified into several types based on their design and functionality. Helical gearmotors feature helical gears for smooth and efficient power transmission, ideal for high-torque applications. Helical-bevel gearmotors combine helical gears with bevel gears, offering versatility in orientation and compact designs. Planetary gearmotors provide high torque in a small package, using a sun-and-planet gear arrangement, while worm gearmotors utilize a screw-like design for high reduction ratios. Other types may include spur or rack-and-pinion gearmotors, catering to varied industrial needs.</p></p>
