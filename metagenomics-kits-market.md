<p><p>The metagenomics kits market comprises three primary types: Library Preparation Kits, Sample Extraction Kits, and Metagenomic Sequencing Kits. Library Preparation Kits are designed to prepare DNA or RNA samples for sequencing by creating a library of fragments. Sample Extraction Kits help isolate microbial DNA or RNA from various environmental samples, ensuring high-quality extraction. Metagenomic Sequencing Kits provide the necessary reagents and protocols for sequencing the prepared metagenomic libraries, enabling researchers to analyze microbial diversity and functions in complex samples.</p></p>
