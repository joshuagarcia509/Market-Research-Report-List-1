<p><strong>The Softgel Drying Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Tumble Drying Systems</li><li>Continuous Tumble Drying Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Softgel Drying Machinery market features two primary types: Standard Tumble Drying Systems and Continuous Tumble Drying Systems. Standard Tumble Drying Systems operate in batch mode, facilitating the drying of softgel capsules in discrete loads, which is ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, Continuous Tumble Drying Systems allow for a steady, uninterrupted flow of softgels through the drying chamber, enhancing efficiency and output. Both systems are essential for ensuring optimal moisture removal, contributing to the quality and shelf life of softgel products.</p></p>
