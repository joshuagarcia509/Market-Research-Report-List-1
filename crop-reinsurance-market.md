<p><p>The crop reinsurance market consists of three primary types: Crop Yield Reinsurance, which protects against losses due to reduced crop yields caused by adverse weather or other factors; Crop Price Reinsurance, which safeguards against declines in the market price of agricultural products; and Crop Revenue Reinsurance, which combines elements of both yield and price insurance, ensuring farmers are compensated for overall revenue loss. These tools help stabilize farmer incomes and promote agricultural resilience in volatile conditions.</p></p>
