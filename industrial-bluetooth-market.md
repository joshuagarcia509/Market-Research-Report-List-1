<p><p>The Industrial Bluetooth market comprises two main types of modules: single-mode and dual-mode. Single-mode modules are designed exclusively for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) applications, offering energy-efficient communication for tasks such as asset tracking and sensor data transmission. In contrast, dual-mode modules support both Bluetooth Classic and BLE, providing flexibility for a wider range of applications, including audio streaming and legacy device connections. This versatility in dual-mode modules allows industries to retain compatibility with older devices while embracing newer technologies.</p></p>
