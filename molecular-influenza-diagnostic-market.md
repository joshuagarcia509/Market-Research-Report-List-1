<p><ul><li>Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR)</li><li>Nucleic Acid Sequence Based Amplification (NASBA) Tests</li><li>Loop-mediated Isothermal Amplification-Based Assays (LAMP)</li><li>Simple Amplification-based Assays (SAMBA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular influenza diagnostic market comprises various advanced testing methods, including Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR), which amplifies viral RNA for precise detection. Nucleic Acid Sequence Based Amplification (NASBA) tests enable amplification at a constant temperature, offering rapid results. Loop-mediated Isothermal Amplification (LAMP) assays provide a fast and efficient alternative by using a single temperature for amplification. Simple Amplification-based Assays (SAMBA) are user-friendly tests that deliver quick and reliable results, facilitating effective influenza diagnosis and management.</p></p>
