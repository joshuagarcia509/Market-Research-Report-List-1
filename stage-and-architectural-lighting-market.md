<p><p>The stage and architectural lighting market is categorized into three main types: LED, Halogen, and Discharge lighting. LED lighting is energy-efficient, durable, and offers versatile color options, making it popular for both stage and architectural applications. Halogen lights provide high-quality illumination and are known for their bright, crisp output but consume more energy. Discharge lighting, including metal halide and high-pressure sodium lamps, is widely used for large venues due to its high intensity and efficiency, suitable for both outdoor and indoor settings.</p></p>
