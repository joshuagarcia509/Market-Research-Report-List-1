<p><strong>The Electric Steering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Steering System market includes three main types: C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering), P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering), and R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering). C-EPS is integrated into the steering column, offering lightweight design and ease of installation. P-EPS is mounted on the pinion gear for improved feedback and precision. R-EPS, positioned on the rack, provides enhanced steering response and efficiency. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements, influencing performance, cost, and driving experience.</p></p>
