<p><strong>The Ventilation Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilation boxes market can be categorized into active and passive systems. </p><p>Active ventilation boxes use mechanical methods, like fans and blowers, to enhance air circulation and ensure efficient air exchange. These systems are typically more effective in controlling indoor air quality, especially in tightly sealed buildings. </p><p>Conversely, passive ventilation boxes leverage natural airflow and pressure differences without mechanical assistance. They rely on architectural design elements, like vents and openings, to maintain air circulation, making them more energy-efficient but potentially less controllable.</p></p>
