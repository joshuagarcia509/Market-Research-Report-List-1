<p><p>The Fabric Hot-Melt Adhesives market comprises two primary types: Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives and Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives are thermoplastic materials that bond fabrics when melted and solidify upon cooling, providing a quick and effective adhesion without chemical reactions. In contrast, Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives involve a chemical reaction during the curing process, resulting in more durable bonds. This distinction allows manufacturers to choose based on application requirements, performance needs, and environmental factors.</p></p>
