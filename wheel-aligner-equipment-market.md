<p><p>The wheel aligner equipment market comprises three primary types: CCD aligners, 3D aligners, and others. CCD aligners use Charge-Coupled Device technology to measure wheel angles, providing precise readings for alignment adjustments. 3D aligners leverage advanced sensors and imaging technology to produce a comprehensive view of the vehicle's alignment in three dimensions, ensuring superior accuracy. Other types may include traditional optical aligners or laser-based systems, catering to diverse customer needs and preferences in vehicle maintenance and repair.</p></p>
