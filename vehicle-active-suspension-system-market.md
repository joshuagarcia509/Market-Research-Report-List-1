<p><p>The Vehicle Active Suspension System market is categorized into three main types of actuators: hydraulic, pneumatic, and electromagnetic. Hydraulic actuators use fluid pressure to adjust suspension, providing strong force and precise control, ideal for heavy vehicles. Pneumatic actuators utilize air pressure for suspension adjustments, offering lightweight and flexible solutions, mainly in commercial vehicles. Electromagnetic actuators leverage electromagnetic forces for rapid and accurate suspension response, enhancing ride comfort and stability, commonly used in luxury and high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
