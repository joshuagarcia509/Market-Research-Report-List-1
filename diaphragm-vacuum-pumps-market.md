<p><strong>The Diaphragm Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Diaphragm Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Two-stage Diaphragm Vacuum Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm vacuum pumps are classified into single-stage and two-stage types based on their design and performance. Single-stage diaphragm vacuum pumps operate with one diaphragm and provide moderate vacuum levels, making them suitable for applications such as laboratory work and light industrial use. In contrast, two-stage diaphragm vacuum pumps utilize two diaphragms to achieve deeper vacuum levels, offering higher efficiency and performance for demanding applications, including pharmaceuticals and electronics manufacturing. The choice between them depends on the required vacuum intensity and application needs.</p></p>
