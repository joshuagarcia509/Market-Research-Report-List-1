<p><ul><li>Magneto Optical Current Transformer (MOCT)</li><li>Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT)</li><li>Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (EFOCT)</li><li>Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FEFOCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical current transformer (OCT) market encompasses various types that leverage optical technology for enhanced performance. Magneto Optical Current Transformers (MOCT) utilize magneto-optical effects for current measurement. Fiber Optical Current Transformers (FOCT) employ optical fibers to transmit current data, ensuring high accuracy and immunity to electromagnetic interference. Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformers (EFOCT) integrate electronic components for signal processing, while Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformers (FEFOCT) offer adaptability for various installations, catering to diverse industrial needs and ensuring precision in power monitoring.</p></p>
