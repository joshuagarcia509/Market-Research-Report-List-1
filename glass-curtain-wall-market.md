<p><strong>The Glass Curtain Wall Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Glazed Type</li><li>Three Glazed Type</li><li>Single Glazed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass curtain wall market consists of three main types: single glazed, double glazed, and triple glazed systems. Single glazed type features a single layer of glass, offering minimal insulation but cost-effectiveness. Double glazed type comprises two glass panes separated by an air or gas-filled space, enhancing thermal performance and reducing energy costs. Triple glazed type includes three layers, providing superior insulation, noise reduction, and energy efficiency, making it ideal for energy-conscious buildings in various climate conditions.</p></p>
