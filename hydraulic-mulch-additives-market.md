<p><p>Hydraulic mulch additives are categorized into two main types based on pH levels: acidic and alkaline. Acidic additives help lower soil pH, enhancing nutrient availability in alkaline soils and promoting better seed germination. Conversely, alkaline additives increase soil pH, beneficial for naturally acidic soils, aiding in nutrient absorption and fostering healthier plant growth. The choice between these types depends on specific soil conditions and desired plant outcomes, influencing agricultural practices and landscape management effectively.</p></p>
