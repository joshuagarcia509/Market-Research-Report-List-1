<p><p>A Power over Ethernet (PoE) Injector is a device used to supply power and data to PoE-enabled devices over standard Ethernet cables. PoE technology simplifies installations by eliminating the need for additional power sources for devices like IP cameras, VoIP phones, and wireless access points. As organizations increasingly adopt smart technologies and IoT solutions, the demand for PoE injectors is rising.</p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) Injector Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include the rising need for efficient networking solutions and the growing trend of smart buildings, which require effective deployment of devices powered via Ethernet. Additionally, advancements in PoE standards, such as PoE+ and PoE++, which deliver higher power levels, are expanding the application areas of PoE technology.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of PoE solutions in various sectors, including healthcare, retail, and transportation, is creating new market opportunities.
