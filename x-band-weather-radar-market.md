<p><strong>The X-Band Weather Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Polarization</li><li>Dual Polarization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Band Weather Radar market is segmented into two main types: Single Polarization and Dual Polarization. Single Polarization radars transmit and receive signals in one polarization mode, making them effective for detecting precipitation but limited in their ability to differentiate between various types. Conversely, Dual Polarization radars transmit and receive signals in both horizontal and vertical polarizations, allowing for enhanced accuracy in identifying particle size, shape, and type, leading to improved weather forecasting and precipitation analysis.</p></p>
