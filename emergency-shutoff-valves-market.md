<p><p>The emergency shutoff valves market primarily includes gate and ball valves, both crucial for controlling fluid flow in critical situations. Gate valves allow for complete shutoff or full flow, providing minimal pressure drop, making them suitable for applications where isolation is needed. Ball valves, on the other hand, offer quick operation and reliable sealing, ideal for systems requiring rapid on/off control. Both types are essential in safeguarding against leaks and ensuring safety in various industrial processes.</p></p>
