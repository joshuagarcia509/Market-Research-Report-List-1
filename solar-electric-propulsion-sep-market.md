<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar Electric Propulsion (SEP) utilizes solar energy to generate thrust for spacecraft, enhancing efficiency and enabling longer missions. The Hall Effect Thruster (HET) is a widely used technology that employs an electric field and magnetic field to accelerate ions for propulsion. The Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT) generates thrust through the periodic release of ionized gas. Other emerging technologies in the SEP market include ion thrusters, arcjets, and Electrospray propulsion, each offering unique advantages for various space applications and mission profiles.</p></p>
