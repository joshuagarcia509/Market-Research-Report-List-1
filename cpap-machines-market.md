<p><strong>The CPAP Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pressure CPAP Device</li><li>Auto Adjusting CPAP Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) machine market features two primary types: Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices and Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices deliver a constant pressure throughout the night, ensuring a steady airflow for users with stable obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices dynamically adjust the pressure based on the user's breathing patterns, providing varying airflow to accommodate changes in airflow resistance and ensuring optimal therapy. Both types play crucial roles in sleep apnea management.</p></p>
