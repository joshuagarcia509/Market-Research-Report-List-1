<p><p>The mini glue gun market features three primary types: high-temperature glue guns, low-temperature glue guns, and dual-temperature glue guns. High-temperature glue guns are ideal for bonding materials like wood and metal, providing strong adhesion for heavy-duty projects. Low-temperature glue guns are suitable for delicate materials, such as fabric and foam, minimizing the risk of burns. Dual-temperature glue guns offer versatility, allowing users to switch between high and low temperatures for various applications, making them a popular choice for crafters and DIY enthusiasts.</p></p>
