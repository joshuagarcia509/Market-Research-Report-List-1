<p><p>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices are medical apparatus designed to provide a constant flow of air to keep the airways open during sleep, primarily used for the treatment of obstructive sleep apnea (OSA). These devices consist of a motor that generates airflow, a hose that delivers the air, and a mask that fits over the nose or mouth.</p><p>The CPAP devices market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of sleep disorders, a rising aging population, and heightened awareness about the importance of sleep health. Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of more compact, user-friendly, and quieter CPAP machines, enhancing patient compliance and satisfaction. Innovations like automatic pressure adjustments and integrated wireless connectivity for monitoring are also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure Devices Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period.
