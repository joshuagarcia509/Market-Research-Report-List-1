<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Web portal software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, are hosted on the provider’s servers and accessed through the internet, offering scalability, reduced upfront costs, and automatic updates while simplifying access for users across different locations.</p></p>
