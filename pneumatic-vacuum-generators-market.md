<p><p>Pneumatic vacuum generators are essential tools in various industries for creating vacuum pressure. The market is primarily divided into single-stage and multi-stage types. Single-stage vacuum generators use a single venturi setup to create a vacuum, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for light-duty applications. In contrast, multi-stage vacuum generators employ multiple venturi stages, enhancing suction strength and efficiency for heavier or more demanding tasks. Each type serves specific needs, influencing factors like performance, size, and complexity in application scenarios.</p></p>
