<p><p>Transient Voltage Suppressors (TVS) are devices designed to protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes. The market for TVS can be categorized into unipolar and bipolar types. Unipolar TVS devices protect against overvoltage in a single polarity, making them suitable for applications with a consistent voltage direction. In contrast, bipolar TVS devices can handle voltage spikes in both polarities, which is essential for AC signals and circuits with fluctuating voltage directions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability of electronic systems.</p></p>
