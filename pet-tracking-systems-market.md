<p><p>Pet tracking systems are categorized into three main types: GPS pet tracking systems utilize satellite technology to provide real-time location data, ideal for outdoor activities and long-range tracking. WiFi pet tracking systems depend on local wireless networks to monitor pets within a defined area, making them suitable for indoor use. Radio pet tracking systems use radio frequency signals for precise tracking but have a limited range, often employed in specific scenarios like hunting or rural settings. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to different tracking needs.</p></p>
