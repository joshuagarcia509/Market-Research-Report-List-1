<p><p>The Nerve Locator/Stimulator market is categorized into two main types: fixed and variable nerve locators/stimulators. Fixed nerve locators provide consistent stimulation output, ensuring reliable identification of peripheral nerves during procedures. In contrast, variable nerve locators allow for adjustable stimulation settings, enabling healthcare professionals to customize the intensity based on individual patient needs or specific anatomical considerations. Both types enhance surgical precision and patient safety by facilitating accurate nerve identification and minimizing potential nerve damage during interventions.</p></p>
