<p><strong>The Benchtop Centrifuge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Benchtop Centrifuge</li><li>Isopycnic Benchtop Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop centrifuge market is categorized into two main types: differential and isopycnic centrifuges. Differential benchtop centrifuges focus on separating particles based on their size and density through varying speeds, allowing for effective sedimentation. In contrast, isopycnic centrifuges separate components based on their buoyant density, achieving stable separation in a uniform gradient. Both types serve essential roles in laboratories for research and clinical applications, enabling precise analysis and purification of biomolecules, cells, and other samples.</p></p>
