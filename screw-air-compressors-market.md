<p><p>The screw air compressor market is primarily categorized into oil-free and oil-injected types. Oil-free screw air compressors are designed for applications requiring clean air, making them ideal for industries like food and pharmaceuticals. They operate without lubrication, reducing contamination risks. In contrast, oil-injected screw air compressors utilize a lubricant to enhance performance and efficiency, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications where air purity is less critical. Both types cater to distinct operational needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
