<p><p>The DOD inkjet printer market is categorized into three main types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ), Drop-On-Demand (DOD), and others. CIJ printers continuously eject ink droplets, suitable for high-speed coding and marking applications. DOD printers release ink droplets only when needed, offering precision and reducing waste, often used in packaging and labeling. Other types may include hybrid models or specialized printers designed for niche applications. Each type serves distinct industrial requirements based on speed, print quality, and application versatility.</p></p>
