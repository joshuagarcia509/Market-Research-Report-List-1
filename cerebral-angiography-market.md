<p><strong>The Cerebral Angiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging</li><li>Computed Tomography</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Nuclear Imaging</li><li>X-ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebral angiography market types include various imaging techniques used to visualize blood vessels in the brain. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed images using magnetic fields and radio waves. Computed Tomography (CT) employs X-rays for cross-sectional brain images. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time imaging of cerebral blood flow. Nuclear Imaging involves radioactive tracers to assess blood circulation. X-ray angiography provides detailed vascular images through X-ray technology, allowing for diagnosis and treatment planning of vascular conditions.</p></p>
