<p><strong>The Sodium Sulfate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Product Sodium Sulfate</li><li>Byproduct Sodium Sulfate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sodium sulfate market is divided into two main types: natural product sodium sulfate and byproduct sodium sulfate. Natural product sodium sulfate is derived from naturally occurring minerals, primarily mined through evaporation of saline water sources. Byproduct sodium sulfate, on the other hand, is produced during the manufacturing processes of other chemicals, particularly from the production of sodium carbonate. Both types serve various applications, including in detergents, glass production, and as a filler in different industries, driving market demand.</p></p>
