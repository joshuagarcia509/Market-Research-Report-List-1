<p><p>The cocoa market comprises three primary segments: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor, obtained from ground cocoa beans, serves as a base for chocolate production. Cocoa butter, the fat extracted from cocoa beans, is valued for its smooth texture and is used in chocolates and cosmetics. Cocoa powder, the residue after extracting cocoa butter, is essential for baking and beverages. Together, these components drive the global cocoa industry, catering to diverse culinary and industrial applications.</p></p>
