<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machine market is segmented into two main types: mechanical CPR machines and automated CPR machines. Mechanical CPR machines provide consistent chest compressions through a manual mechanism, ensuring a rhythmic compressive force. In contrast, automated CPR machines utilize advanced technology to deliver precise, adjustable compressions without human intervention. Both types aim to enhance the quality of CPR delivery during cardiac emergencies, improving patient outcomes by maintaining blood flow until advanced medical help is available.</p></p>
