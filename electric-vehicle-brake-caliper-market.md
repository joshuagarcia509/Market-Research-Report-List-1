<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Brake Caliper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Brake Caliper Market offers two main types: single piston and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize a single hydraulic piston to apply pressure on the brake pads, providing a lightweight and cost-effective solution, ideal for smaller electric vehicles. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons, allowing for more even pad wear and improved braking performance, making them suitable for high-performance electric vehicles that require superior stopping power and heat management.</p></p>
