<p><strong>The Three Phase Pelletizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-Screw</li><li>Single-Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase pelletizers market is primarily divided into two types: twin-screw and single-screw pelletizers. Twin-screw pelletizers utilize two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, enhanced control, and superior processing of various materials, making them ideal for complex formulations. Single-screw pelletizers, on the other hand, utilize a single screw mechanism, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for standard applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, such as pharmaceuticals, food, and plastics, each serving specific processing needs.</p></p>
