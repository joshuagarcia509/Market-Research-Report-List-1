<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) Chipsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Chipset</li><li>PoE Powered Devices (PD) Chipset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) chipsets market is divided into two main categories: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) chipsets and Powered Devices (PD) chipsets. PSE chipsets are responsible for delivering power over Ethernet cables to devices, facilitating network connectivity and power for devices like IP cameras and wireless access points. In contrast, PD chipsets are integrated into the end devices that receive power, enabling them to operate without needing a separate power source. Together, they enhance energy efficiency and simplify installation.</p></p>
